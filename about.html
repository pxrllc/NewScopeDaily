About Global News Perspectives

AI-Driven Multi-Perspective News Aggregator

Project Goal

This experimental project aims to visualize how global events are reported differently across regions. By aggregating RSS feeds from major international broadcasters and using AI to summarize key topics, we provide a daily digest that highlights diverse viewpoints from the "Global South" alongside Western perspectives.

Data Sources & RSS Usage

News content is aggregated from publicly available RSS feeds. Detailed attribution (original title, source link, publication date) is provided for every item.

  • Associated Press (AP) - United States
  • Reuters - United Kingdom / Global
  • BBC News - United Kingdom (World, Africa, Asia, Middle East, Latin America)
  • Deutsche Welle (DW) - Germany
  • Al Jazeera - Qatar (Middle East)
  • Global Voices - International (Citizen Media)
  • Euronews - Pan-European
  • The Guardian - United Kingdom

Disclaimer: This service does not claim ownership of any news content. Summaries are generated by AI and may contain inaccuracies. Please refer to original articles for verification.
